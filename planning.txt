Player Class:
  - Keeps track of player information
    - lives
    - name

  - Actions:
    - reduce number of lives

Question Class:
  state:
    - question
    - solution

  Action:
    - Asks a question
      - Takes an answer (evaluates)
      - Displays results (correct/incorrect)

Game Class:
  state:
    - player
  
  Actions:
    - ask a question
    - display results
    - Determine winner
